ü§î Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about your learning adventure

Q

How do I get started with Gyaanoo?

Simply create your free account, choose your child's grade level, and customize their avatar. Then explore the six magical learning worlds at your own pace! Our getting started tutorial will guide you through every step.

Q

What devices are supported?

Gyaanoo works on tablets, laptops, Chromebooks, and interactive whiteboards. We support all modern browsers including Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge. Our mobile app is available for iOS and Android devices.

Q

How do I track my child's progress?

The Parent Command Center provides detailed analytics, achievement tracking, and progress reports. You'll receive weekly summaries and can set learning goals together with your child. Real-time notifications keep you updated on milestones.

Q

Is Gyaanoo safe for children?

Absolutely! We're COPPA compliant with robust privacy protections. All interactions are moderated, no personal information is shared, and parents have full control over privacy settings. Our platform is designed with child safety as the top priority.

Q

Can teachers use Gyaanoo in classrooms?

Yes! Our Teacher Mission Control provides classroom management tools, curriculum alignment, and group activities. Teachers can create assignments, track class progress, and facilitate collaborative learning experiences.
